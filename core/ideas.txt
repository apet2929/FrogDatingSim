Frog Dating Sim:


You are a frog racer
You race for money and success and fame and the ladies
You wear a fedora.
You give the ladies a lily on a lilypad.

Gameplay:
    - top down 2d rpg overworld
        - talk to da ladies
        - pre-scripted cutscenes
        - pick up items like clothes to make you look cool
        - pre-date chatter

    - action state
        - Racing
        - You have a bunch of opponent frogs (I would have to make AI which would be challenging)
        - You move horizontally slowly
        - You can jump really high
        - You can swing from branch to branch with your tongue
